About Pm Internship

The PM Internship platform is a flagship initiative by the Government of India designed to bridge the gap between talented students and public service opportunities. We believe that by providing a single, accessible platform, we can empower the youth of India to contribute to nation-building through meaningful internships in various government ministries and departments.

Our mission is to democratize access to high-quality government internships, making it easier for students from all backgrounds to find and apply for positions that align with their skills and aspirations. We leverage technology to offer a seamless and transparent application process, ensuring that every student has an equal opportunity to thrive.
